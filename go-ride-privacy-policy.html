Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Welcome to GoRide. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our mobile applications (Passenger App & Driver App) and related services.

1. Information We Collect

a) Personal Information

  • Name
  • Mobile number
  • Email address
  • Profile photo (optional)
  • Government ID (for drivers – PAN, Aadhaar, License)

b) Location Information

We collect your real-time location to provide ride-related services:

  • Passenger: pickup, drop, and live tracking
  • Driver: online/offline tracking, ride assignment, navigation

c) Usage & Device Information

  • Device model, OS version
  • IP address
  • App activity logs
  • Crash reports

d) Payment Information

Payments are handled securely via Razorpay / Paytm / UPI. We do not store card/UPI information.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To match passengers with nearby drivers
  • To provide navigation and trip tracking
  • To process payments and generate invoices
  • For customer support
  • For safety, fraud prevention, and analytics

3. Sharing of Information

We may share your information with:

  • Drivers and passengers during an active ride
  • Payment gateways (Razorpay / Paytm)
  • Law enforcement agencies (if legally required)
  • Service providers for analytics or hosting

4. Location Permission (Android/iOS)

We use:

  • Foreground location – for ride tracking
  • Background location (Driver App) – to update live location

5. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active or required by law.

6. Security

We follow industry-standard security practices to protect your data.

7. Your Rights

  • Access your data
  • Update or correct information
  • Request deletion (subject to legal obligations)

8. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 18 years of age.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted here.
