Child Safety Standards Policy — Task Grid

Last updated: August 21, 2025

Task Grid is committed to child safety and compliance with Google Play’s Child Safety Standards and all applicable laws.

1) Scope & Audience

  • Task Grid is not directed to children and is intended for users 18+.
  • We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has used our service, please contact us at sinhansaitparkps@gmail.com so we can take appropriate action, including deletion of related data.

2) Zero-Tolerance for CSAM

We have zero tolerance for child sexual abuse material (CSAM) or any content that sexualizes or exploits minors, including text, images, videos, links, grooming, or solicitation. We promptly remove such content and report it to the appropriate authorities where required by law.

3) User-Generated Content & Moderation

(If Task Grid does not contain user-generated content, you may remove this section.)

  • Prohibited: CSAM; sexualization of minors; grooming; solicitation of minors; harassment or threats toward minors; attempts to bypass safety controls.
  • Enforcement: Content removal; account suspension/termination; device/IP restrictions; reporting to authorities as appropriate.
  • Review: Reports are prioritized. We use a combination of automated and manual review to detect violations.

4) Reporting Child-Safety Concerns

If you encounter content or behavior that may endanger a child:

  • Email: sinhansaitparkps@gmail.com
  • In-app: Use the “Report” or “Contact Support” option (if available)
  • Please include relevant details (screenshots, profile links, timestamps) to help us act quickly.

5) Cooperation with Authorities

Where legally required or necessary to protect a child, we will preserve information and report to competent authorities or authorized hotlines, consistent with applicable laws and due process.

6) Data Handling

We minimize data collection and do not knowingly process children’s data. Upon verified notice of a child’s unauthorized use, we will delete associated data.

7) Consequences of Violations

We may remove content, restrict features, or terminate accounts that violate this policy without prior notice.
